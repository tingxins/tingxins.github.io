1. 适用范围

本隐私政策适用于 iRepeater iOS 应用以及为该应用提供的静态法律页面。它不适用于你通过系统分享表、邮件客户端、App Store、Apple 服务或其他第三方服务单独提交给对应服务方的信息。

iRepeater 当前不提供用户登录、在线素材托管、远程备份或社区发布能力。你导入的音频文件由系统文件选择、AirDrop 或文件共享等本地路径进入应用沙盒，应用不会把音频内容上传到我们的服务器。

3. 产品分析的边界

iRepeater 的产品分析通过本地的 TXAnalytics 门面进入 AmplitudeSwift。应用代码会在事件离开设备前过滤文件名、完整路径、原始搜索词和音频内容，并优先使用分桶值。

可能发送的示例字段包括：

screen_nameentry_pointresultmaterial_typeduration_bucketsegment_count_bucketquery_length_bucketplayback_rate

这些字段用于理解功能是否可用、流程是否顺畅和哪些训练能力需要改进。我们不会为了产品分析上传你的音频内容、文件名、完整路径或原始搜索词。

4. 第三方服务

当前版本使用 AmplitudeSwift 进行产品分析。Amplitude 可能接收产品分析事件、会话信息、应用生命周期信息、设备或安装标识和应用版本等技术信息。我们使用这些信息的目的限于产品改进、稳定性观察和问题排查。

App 也可能通过 Apple 的系统能力完成安装、更新、文件导入、邮件发送和系统备份等操作。对于 Apple 或你设备上其他应用独立处理的数据，请以对应服务方的政策为准。

5. 你的控制方式

  • 你可以在 App 设置中关闭分析开关。关闭后，iRepeater 不再主动发送产品分析事件。
  • 你可以删除本地音频素材；删除素材不会自动删除已经保存的学习记录，但缺少本地文件时相关记录可能无法播放。
  • 你可以在设置中清除学习记录，包括最近播放、收藏片段和已保存片段。
  • 你可以通过 irepeater@midaigc.com 联系我们，提出访问、更正或删除与你主动反馈相关的信息的请求。

6. 保留与删除

本地音频、学习记录、播放进度和偏好会保留在设备内，直到你删除素材、清除记录、卸载应用或系统清理相关数据。由于这些数据主要在设备内处理，我们通常无法远程访问或恢复它们。

产品分析数据由 Amplitude 根据其服务规则保留。我们会尽量限制发送字段，只保留改进产品所需的概括性使用信息。你主动发送给我们的邮件反馈会按处理问题和维护沟通所需的时间保留。

7. 安全措施

iRepeater 依赖 iOS 沙盒、本地文件系统和系统文件选择能力来隔离应用数据。我们在产品分析前进行字段过滤，避免把本地素材名称、路径和原始搜索内容作为分析字段发送。

没有任何安全措施能保证绝对安全。请只导入你有权使用且愿意保存在本设备上的音频文件，并根据需要自行做好设备级备份与访问保护。

8. 未成年人

iRepeater 面向一般语言学习和听说训练场景，并不主动面向儿童收集资料。若你认为未成年人向我们发送了不适合保留的反馈内容，请通过 irepeater@midaigc.com 联系我们处理。

9. 政策变更

当 iRepeater 的功能、数据处理方式、第三方服务或平台要求发生变化时，我们会更新本政策，并调整页面顶部的生效日期。重大变化会尽量通过 App 内入口或发布说明提示。

如需咨询本政策，请发送邮件至 irepeater@midaigc.com。

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the iRepeater iOS app and the static legal pages provided for the app. It does not apply to information you separately provide to Apple, your email client, the system share sheet, App Store services, or other third-party services.

iRepeater currently does not provide user login, online material hosting, remote backup, or community publishing features. Audio files you import enter the app sandbox through local system flows such as the document picker, AirDrop, or file sharing. The app does not upload audio content to our servers.

3. Analytics boundary

iRepeater sends product analytics through the local TXAnalytics facade to AmplitudeSwift. Before events leave the device, app code filters file names, full paths, raw search text, and audio content, and prefers bucketed values.

Example fields may include:

screen_nameentry_pointresultmaterial_typeduration_bucketsegment_count_bucketquery_length_bucketplayback_rate

These fields help us understand whether features work, where flows are difficult, and which training capabilities need improvement. We do not upload your audio content, file names, full paths, or raw search text for product analytics.

4. Third-party services

The current version uses AmplitudeSwift for product analytics. Amplitude may receive product analytics events, session information, app lifecycle information, device or installation identifiers, app version, and similar technical information. We use this information only for product improvement, stability observation, and troubleshooting.

The app may also use Apple system capabilities for installation, updates, file import, email sending, and system backup. For data independently handled by Apple or other apps on your device, please refer to the policies of those service providers.

5. Your controls

  • You can turn off analytics in app Settings. After that, iRepeater stops actively sending product analytics events.
  • You can delete local audio materials. Deleting materials does not automatically delete saved learning records, but records may not be playable without the local file.
  • You can clear learning records in Settings, including recent playback, favorite clips, and saved clips.
  • You can contact us at irepeater@midaigc.com to request access, correction, or deletion of information related to feedback you sent us.

6. Retention and deletion

Local audio, learning records, playback progress, and preferences stay on the device until you delete materials, clear records, uninstall the app, or the system removes related data. Because this data is mainly processed on device, we generally cannot remotely access or restore it.

Product analytics data is retained by Amplitude under its service rules. We limit the fields we send and keep analytics focused on summarized information needed to improve the product. Email feedback you send us is retained only as needed to handle the issue and maintain the conversation.

7. Security

iRepeater relies on the iOS sandbox, the local file system, and system file-selection capabilities to isolate app data. We filter analytics fields before sending product analytics, so local material names, paths, and raw search text are not sent as analytics fields.

No security measure is absolute. Please import only audio files you have the right to use and are comfortable keeping on this device, and keep your own device-level backups and access protection as needed.

8. Children

iRepeater is intended for general language learning and listening/speaking practice. It does not intentionally collect information from children. If you believe a child sent us feedback that should not be retained, contact us at irepeater@midaigc.com.

9. Changes

If iRepeater features, data practices, third-party services, or platform requirements change, we will update this policy and the effective date above. For significant changes, we will try to provide notice through the app or release notes.

Questions about this policy can be sent to irepeater@midaigc.com.
